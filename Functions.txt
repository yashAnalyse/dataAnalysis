\f0\fs24 \cf0 Data Analysis with Python - Pandas\
\
1. read_csv() : to read from csv file\
2. head() : will give first five rows of data\
3. Shape : will give total no of rows, columns\
4. Columns : will give list of columns\
5. Types : will give data types of each column\
6. unique() : will give us all the unique values in a particular column\
7. nunique() : will give us total no of unique values present in a particular column or in the entire data frame\
8. count() : will give us total entities present in a data frame \
9. isnull() : will give us true or false based on the value (if value is not null then true otherwise false)\
10. sum() : will give us the total \
11. value_counts() : will give us no of instances with unique value
